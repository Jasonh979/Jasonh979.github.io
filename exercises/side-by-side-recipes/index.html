TACO SALAD

PREP TIME: 15 MINUTES COOK TIME: 15 MINUTES TOTAL TIME: 15 MINTUES YIELDS: 2 to 4

Ever get that taco craving but want a lighter, fresher option? This Taco Salad is your answer with all the flavors and textures of a taco but with veggies as the base. From he crunchy cheesy chips to the zesty chipotle sauce and seasoned ground beef, this quick and easy yet satisfying meal will be a hit. It basically tastes like Taco Bell in a big old salad bowl!
