THE BEST MAC & CHEESE

 PREP TIME: 30 MIN    COOK TIME: 45 MIN    TOTAL TIME: 1 HR 15 MIN   YIELDS 6-8


INGREDIENTS

PASTA

  • 1 pound large elbow macaroni
  • salt, to cook pasta

CHEESE SAUCE

  • -6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • -3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • -1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • -3 3/4 cup warm heavy cream
  • - 3 tablespoons cream cheese, softened
  • -1 1/2 cup sharp cheddar, shredded
  • -1 cup Colby jack, shredded
  • -1/2 cup smoked gouda, shredded
  • -2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • -1 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard powder
  • -1/2 teaspoon black pepper more to taste
  • -1/2 to 1 teaspoon salt, to taste
  • -pinch of nutmeg
  • -pinch of cayenne

OTHER

  • -2 cups shredded cheese, of choice
  • -optional: chopped parsley or chives and grated parmesan on top

INSTRUCTIONS:

COOK THE PASTA

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook a minute below al dente. The pasta will finish cooking in the oven.

MAKE THE CHEESE SAUCE

  1. Heat a saucepan or pot over medium heat. Add the butter. Once melted, whisk in the flour and cook for 2 to 3 minutes until it just starts to bubble and turns a yellow-golden color.
  2. Add in the garlic and saute with the roux for another minute.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream slowly and whisk continuously until smooth. Then add the cream cheese, dry mustard powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, nutmeg, cayenne powder, and salt. Whisk together until smooth. Continue to cook the sauce for 3 to 4 minutes or until it thickens.
  4. Reduce the heat to low or take off the heat and add the sharp cheddar, Colby jack, and smoked gouda. Whisk together until there are no more clumps and the sauce is completely smooth. Taste and adjust anything to your liking.

ASSEMBLY & BAKING

  1. Once the pasta is done cooking, drain it and toss it into the sauce immediately. Mix together in a large baking dish.
  2. Once the pasta is done cooking, drain it and toss it into the sauce immediately. Mix together in a large baking dish.
  3. Bake at 350째F for 15 minutes. Then turn to broil for a few minutes just until the top is golden and crispy - make sure to monitor it closely.
  4. Top it off with chopped parsley or chives and grated parmesan and enjoy!

CROISSANT EGG BOATS

 PREP TIME: 15 MIN    COOK TIME: 20 MIN    TOTAL TIME: 35 MIN   YIELDS 8


INGREDIENTS

  • 8 Croissants

BACON AND CHEDDAR

  • -6 Large eggs
  • -6 Strips of bacon
  • -1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • -salt, to taste
  • - black pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS:

PREPARE THE CROISSANTS:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C).
  2. Slice the top off each croissant and gently carve out the center creating a boat-like shape. Be careful not to cut through the bottom or sides.
  3. Press down the remaining center of the croissant to create a well for the fillings.

BACON AND CHEDDAR

  1. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese into the well of each of 4 croissants.
  2. Place a strip of beef bacon on top of the cheese in the well.
  3. Crack an egg into each croissant boat, directly on top of the bacon and cheese.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  5. Add a little bit more cheese on top.
  6. Place the filled croissants on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the croissants are golden.

LOADED TATER TOT CUPS

 PREP TIME: 15 MIN    COOK TIME: 30 MIN    TOTAL TIME: 45 MIN   YIELDS 12 CUPS


INGREDIENTS

  • 1 bag frozen tater tots
  • avocado oil spray
  • sour cream
  • beef bacon, chopped
  • green onions or chives, thinly sliced
  • cheddar cheese, shredded

Garlic Butter

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon parsley, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons parmesan, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon dried oregano

INSTRUCTIONS:

MAKE THE GARLIC BUTTER

  1. In a small bowl, mix butter with parsley, garlic, parmesan, salt, black pepper, and oregano.

MAKE THE CUPS

  1. Preheat the oven to 425째F.
  2. Brush each cup in a cupcake tray with garlic butter or just spray with avocado oil.
  3. Add 5 to 6 tater tots into each cup and bake for 10 minutes.
  4. Take out of the oven and use a shot glass to mash the tater tots into a cup shape.
  5. Brush the inside with more garlic butter and add a little bit of shredded cheddar cheese inside each cup
  6. Place the filled croissants on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the croissants are golden.
  7. Pop back in the oven and bake for 13 to 17 minutes until golden and crispy all around.
  8. Take out of the oven, allow to cool for 5 minutes, and then slide out the cups.
  9. Add sour cream, beef bacon, green onions, and more cheese into each cup and serve warm!
